13th Photovoltaic Science, Application and Technology Conference

PVSAT-13

5 - 7 April 2017

Bangor University, UK

Organized by The Solar Energy Society (UK-ISES).

PVSAT is the UK’s premier scientific event for solar PV where the latest advances in UK research and development are presented and discussed. This 3-day conference covers all PV technologies; crystalline silicon, inorganic thin film, multi-junction concentrators and emerging organic and hybrid cells, including perovskite cells.

A draft programme for the conference is now available to download below. This programme is subject to change.

Dinorwig Hydro Power Station Tour

Wed 5 April (Pickup at 8.30 AM)

Set against a magnificent backdrop of the towering mountains and the magnificent scenery of Snowdonia, First Hydro's pumped storage power station in Dinorwig, Llanberis supplies electricity to the Grid on a daily basis and provides back-up for periods of heavy demand. The station offers a seriously fast response time - probably the fastest of any power facility in the world - 1,728MW from standstill in just 90 seconds!

Don't miss this amazing opportunity for a tour of Dinorwig Power Station, available to PVSAT-13 delegates only.

To register, please email stevie.scanlan@bangor.ac.uk. The tour is limited to 28 places so register quick!
