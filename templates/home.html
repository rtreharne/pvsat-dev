13th Photovoltaic Science, Application and Technology Conference

PVSAT-13

5 - 7 April 2017

Bangor University, UK

Organized by The Solar Energy Society (UK-ISES).

PVSAT is the UK’s premier scientific event for solar PV where the latest advances in UK research and development are presented and discussed. This 3-day conference covers all PV technologies; crystalline silicon, inorganic thin film, multi-junction concentrators and emerging organic and hybrid cells, including perovskite cells.

The EPSRC Supergen Supersolar Hub are providing a number of bursaries to students wishing to attend PVSAT-13. Students wishing to apply for a bursary should click the button below.

Research Methods in Solar PV

University of Bangor

A one day workshop in the field of PV ideal for early stage researchers, PhD students, industry workers or postdocs. organised by the EPSRC SuperSolar PV Hub in association with PVSAT.
