Apply for a Student Bursary

A total of 10 student bursaries are being provided by the EPSRC Supergen SuperSolar consortium for students wishing to attend PVSAT-13. Click the button below to download the application form.

The bursary will cover the cost of the student delegate conference registration fee. No funds will be granted to meet travel, accommodation or sustenance costs, these will remain the responsibility of the Student in receipt of a bursary. Bursaries will be limited to one student per institution. Completed forms should be returned to info@uk-ises.org.

Recipients of bursaries will be required to register as members of the SuperSolar Hub (for free).
